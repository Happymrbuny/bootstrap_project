Your Favorite Beers

Bent Paddle

Black Ale

My favorite beer of all time. Smooth, malty, easy to drink. There is a very subtle sweetness to this beer, but it won't leave you feeling like you have been snacking on sweets all night. It does have some heft to it, so you might fill up on just a couple of them.

Surly

Furious IPA

This beer is really hoppy. If you want to get the most flavor possible out of your beer, this is the one. It can get on top of you though, since it has a high ABV, and will distroy any other flavors you are trying to enjoy.

Summit

Extra Pale Ale

I really like this beer. Easy to drink without overwhelming the flavor of any food you are having along with this beer. Refreshing in the summer, cozy in the winter. Good for any time of the day!
